About LDF

Lama Development Foundation (LDF) is a community initiative led by young people from Lama, Bandarban. Our mission is to accelerate local prosperity through green livelihoods, education, women-led enterprises and resilient health services.

Vision

A thriving Lama with shared prosperity, clean environment and equal opportunity for all.

Mission

Enable community-driven projects, skills and enterprises that create dignified jobs while protecting nature.

Programs

Lama Green Hub

Mushroom cultivation training, solar micro-solutions, eco-blocks & weekly CSA produce boxes.

Women Empowerment

Market-aligned skills and micro-entrepreneurship for women and adolescent girls.

Education & Skills

After-school learning, career mentoring and job-ready digital skills for youth.

Sustainable Tourism

Community-led eco-tours creating income while conserving Lama’s natural beauty.

Green Lama

Segregation at source, upcycling crafts and municipal partnerships for cleaner streets.

Community Health

Low-cost tests, health camps and emergency coordination with local providers.

Current Projects

Mushroom Starter Lab

Hands-on training & starter kits for 50 families.

Solar for Study

Portable solar lights for students in off-grid hamlets.

Waste-to-Blocks

Eco-block prototypes using local waste and sand mix.
